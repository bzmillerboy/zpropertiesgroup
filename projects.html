Reading Apt. Building Renovation

4829 Reading Rd., Cincinnati, OH, 45237

Comments: Z Properties purchased this building in September of 2009 and immediately started in on the renovations with the support of Ruehlman Construction. The building got all new hot water heaters, baseboard heat, an electric furnace, updated electric and new plumbing. From there, the interior remodeling consisted of drywall work, paint, new electric outlets and light fixtures, new plumbing fixtures, beautiful hardwood floors, brand new gas ranges and refrigerators, updated bathrooms, new HVAC and more. We were able to create a modern feel but still maximized the impressive architecutre in the building with the soaring 9' ceilings, exquisite molding and impressive fireplaces. The interior work was completed in November of 2009 and has been occupied with very satisfied tenants ever since. The exterior work has been started and will be completed in March 2010. JJ Extreme Exteriors is completing a body of work which entails new soffits, siding on dormers and gables, painting all exterior trim, new fence and updating the detached 3 car garage. This building is a a great location that has alot of development activity around it. The building is 1 block from the proposed 42M train depot project in Norwood!
